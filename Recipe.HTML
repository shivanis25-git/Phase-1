Paneer Butter masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy, and flavorful Indian curry made with soft paneer cubes cooked in a buttery tomato sauce. It is one of the most loved dishes in North India!

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 cup Cream
  • Salt and Spices as per taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Blend the mixture into a smooth paste.
  4. Return the paste to the pan, add spices, and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cream, mix gently.
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes and serve hot with naan or rice.

Tip: For extra flavor, add a small spoon of kasuri methi before serving!
